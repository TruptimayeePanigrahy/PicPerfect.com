About Me

I love music, cooking, filmmaking, photography, and my beautiful family! I have been taking photos my whole life but decided to do it professionally in 2010 and I love it more every day. I am very much a people person but ironically my specialty is photographing products, food, and beverages. Just because I specialize in capturing things doesn’t mean I lack in portrait photography, the only thing I don’t shoot is weddings ;)
